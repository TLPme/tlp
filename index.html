Statement on AI

I never compete with AI. I direct it.

Clients hire me to leverage discernment — knowing what to keep, what to cut, what to question, and what not to publish. I determine the risk calibration, ethical framing, tonal control/voice, and final judgment.

Here's how I do that.

I use Claude and ChatGPT for structural development and counterargument testing, Perplexity for research synthesis across complex topics, and AI-assisted tools for fact-checking and terminology verification when writing spans technical domains.

"AI writing" bores me and will bore your audience. Instead, let me give them informed, strategically engineered communication shaped by my human judgment.
